Rodha - Skill Building Game

Students navigate a series of obstacles by using the environment and game mechanics to move and jump over gaps. Success requires understanding the game’s physics and experimenting with different strategies to progress.

  • Grade Levels: grades 3+
  • Skills Developed: problem solving, timing, strategic planning, and spatial awareness
  • Standards Addressed:
    Common Core Mathematical Practices - make sense of problems and persevere in solving them by using physics mechanics to move through levels, look for and make use of structure by recognizing patterns
  • Classroom Use:
    Brain Break - the game’s physics-based mechanics and puzzle solving challenges provide a stimulating yet fun way for students to reset their focus
    Reflection Activity - students reflect on their strategies and how they adapted their approach to overcome obstacles throughout the game
At each level, the goal is to reach the portal safely. Use the arrow keys to hop left and right. Or you can control the hero with A and D keys. Press again for a double jump while you're in the air. Can you pass all 60 levels?
